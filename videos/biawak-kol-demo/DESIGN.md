# Design System

## Overview

Biawak Kol is a mobile-first sports leaderboard app with a clean white operating surface and a restrained admin-dashboard layout. The identity is utilitarian and community-focused: a left navigation rail, rounded statistic cards, compact tables, avatar initials, and a strong green primary action for recording games. The production capture centers on July 2026 data, where real leaderboard rows and player photos provide the visual proof. Motion should feel quick, organized, and scorekeeping-native rather than cinematic fantasy.

## Colors

- **Primary Surface**: `#FFFFFF` - main app background and card surfaces.
- **Primary Text**: `#0A0A0A` - table labels, stat values, and primary copy.
- **Secondary Text**: `#737373` - muted descriptions and card labels.
- **Quiet Border**: `#E5E5E5` - table dividers, cards, and navigation separation.
- **Soft Panel**: `#F4F4F5` - neutral chips and subtle panel fills.
- **Brand Green**: `#008236` - primary CTA, active navigation, winning accents.
- **Qualified Green Tint**: `#F0FDF4` - supportive green highlight field.
- **Alert Red Text**: `#82181A` - lower table zone / under-qualified rows.
- **Alert Red Tint**: `#FFE2E2` - under-qualified row background.
- **Gold Accent**: `#D08700` - rank and award emphasis.

## Typography

- **Primary UI**: Inter Variable (100-900). Used for all app labels, tables, buttons, and numbers.
- **Display / Data**: DM Sans Variable (100-1000). Used in video for large stat callouts and demo captions.
- **Brand Accent**: Allura (400). Use sparingly as a small human accent, never for dense UI.
- **Hierarchy**: hero numbers 112-176px, scene titles 58-92px, UI/table text 20-34px, labels 16-22px. Use tabular numbers for stats.

## Elevation

Depth is created with thin borders, white cards, subtle shadows, and tonal panels. The app avoids heavy shadows and uses rounded corners around 10-18px. Video compositions should add depth through layered screenshots, table cards, avatar/photo stacks, and small green/gold highlight glows while keeping the underlying interface crisp.

## Components

- **Left Navigation Rail**: fixed white rail with logo, black icon labels, and a rounded green active pill.
- **Monthly Stat Cards**: four compact rounded cards for total games, player count, top player, and win rate.
- **Leaderboard Table**: dense score table with rank, avatar, name, games, wins, losses, points, coefficient, and win percentage.
- **Win Percentage Pills**: small neutral chips for percentages, red-tinted chips in the lower qualification zone.
- **Primary Record CTA**: green rounded button labeled "Catat Game Baru".
- **Player Photo Avatars**: real player image assets and circular initials used as human context.
- **Award / MVP Surface**: trophy-driven player-recognition mode connected to captured player imagery.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use the exact white, green, neutral, red-tint, and gold palette from the capture.
- Keep UI panels dense, aligned, and table-driven.
- Use real production names and numbers: July 2026, eleven games, twenty five players, Ko Yusin, eighty six percent.
- Let rows cascade in with short leaderboard-style timing.
- Use player photos and screenshots as framed assets with slow pans, not static flat images.

### Don'ts

- Do not make the video dark, neon, or fantasy-styled.
- Do not replace the captured data with placeholder names.
- Do not overuse mascot imagery; the app UI and records are the product.
- Do not use large rounded marketing cards inside other cards.
- Do not make typography decorative where table readability matters.
